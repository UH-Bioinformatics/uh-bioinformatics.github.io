The bioinformatics Core provides services to researchers throughout the University of Hawaii System on a fee-for-service or cost recovery basis. Our expertise spans various subfields of bioinformatics, including NGS data analysis and machine learning, allowing us to help researchers tackle the analysis of popular protocols or conceive custom de-novo pipelines and tools. For more information, please refer to our services section or contact us.

Also, you can sign up to receive updates from the Core, including workshop announcements, available tools and blog posts.
